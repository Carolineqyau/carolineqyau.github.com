ECO

Challenge

Planned obsolescence - a lot of products are planned to break so that companies can sell more to the consumers. This creates a lot of waste and gives a financial burden to the consumers on overbuying.If we could encourage consumers to purchase products that have good quality and sustainable, there would be much less waste and the products would cost less in the long run for the consumers because it lasts.

Solution

Eco is an app that decreases irresponsible consumption and waste by providing product comparison services, product ranking and rating based on sustainability, quality and price that consumers can search for during shopping using keyword searches and photo recognition of the products.

The products that we help our users pick are not only good for the environment, but also better for helping our users save money because of the good quality.

Eco also has a social feature called the green thumb ranking that the users can share and invite friends and family to join, as well as reward users with positive words when they make sustainable purchases.
